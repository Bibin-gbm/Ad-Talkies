Eat healthily, Live healthily.

14 Jan 2019

The food is not just a word, it's the synonym of our life. All beings living or hardworking for their hungry. So we should eat good and healthy food. But can we? We know we are eating high toxic food and beverages. But we are helpless. So every day we eating slow poison knowingly as well as those poisons we giving to our kids by the name of Food. This is not the time to worried about the situation. We have to do something for us and for the future generation. If you have the little space, do your own farming. We can't cultivate everything but how much we can, that much we have to do! But now we have a glad news "Aan's Integrated Organic Farm".

Pure organic food suppliers in Trivandrum. Trivandrum is the blessed land by the Arabian sea, Rivers, Lake and the Western Ghats, now onwards those blessing will get us as the pure healthy food on every home doorstep in Trivandrum. Organic Vegetables, Pearl spot and Tilapia fishes, Non Pasteurized pure milk of Goat and Cow, Fruits, Eggs and etc... all edible products for a healthy life here from we get.

Aan's integrated farm has some speciality. First one is, fresh farm products they will deliver to every home's doorstep in the urban area of Trivandrum. Second, they have been cultivating a special range of papaya and Pisang lilin bananas for diabetic patients, and for cholesterol, patients BV-380 brown eggs. Because Aan's Integrated Farm's has a motto, fill your belly through healthy food. That's why they serve 100% pure organic food. And also usually, we know soil type is the main thing for the good farming. So Aan's Integrated farm carefully chose their farmland. Yes, Aan's Integrated Farm situating in Aryanad. The beautiful valley of western ghats. We can say more precisely valley of Agasthya Mountain. The mysterious magical medicinal mountain in the land of Sree Padmanabha. Their nutritious soil will nourish our health with good healthy food.

Feel the goodness of our nature through the pure healthy foods with Aan's Integrated Organic Farm.
