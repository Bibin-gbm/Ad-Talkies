Weaving your dream with aromatic heritage.

27 Oct 2018

Paarijaatham, a beautiful name granted from our heritage. It’s sound spreading beautiful heavenly aroma in us. paarijaatham had to say a beautiful love story behind the reason of its arrivals from heaven to earth. A story from Dwaparyug. We cannot forget our tradition and heritage. That is we still keeping those aromatic rich heritage with us. Here, Our traditional wear blending with our traditional flower name Paarijaatham. Paarijaatham is the heritage hub of our waving culture, as well it’s the hub of our traditional wear. An only wear in the world can demand their rich pedigree... That is Saree. This long rich and ethnic wear can demand many things, such as an outfit voyages from prehistoric time to present with the unfeigned glory, and the only wear that changes according to our different situations from classy to trendy. It’s colouring different emotional shades in us through the resplendent colours as well with its wrapping style. Classic, Ethnic, Trendy etc.... Saree's long story tells here through our another fragrant heritage boon paarijaatham.

We are going to explore our weaving culture within a few minutes. Here clubbing our 29 state’s sarees under in one roof, that is Paarijaatham's one of the speciality. A unique boutique only for sarees. This sound is traditional but behind the story of Parijatham is young and inspirational. A group of young IT professionals begun this entrepreneurship in 2015 by the door to door saree sale from Nagercoil. Then they revert to online sale. Now it flourished as boutiques from Nagercoil and to Trivandrum. They going to start soon two new boutiques in Bangalore and Hyderabad. Now Paarijaatham spreading its mesmerising soothing aroma to the whole over of south India. This unique boutique is the symbol of willpower and enthusiastic minds. Here they carefully picked beautiful sarees. Such as different type of silk, cotton, Linen sarees. South Indians are proud to with our own traditional discover Kancheepuram silks. That unique heritage we can customize from here. Choose your Kancheepuram silk thread, and make your own signature on your precious Kancheepuram silk saree. You can also customize every sarees and you can grab other unique sarees such as linen and other handlooms cottons. Also here have variety Kurtis and churidar set collections.

One day you must come to paarijaatham. and buy your own saree that’s only made for you.
